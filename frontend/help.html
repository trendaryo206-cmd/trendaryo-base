Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days. International shipping varies by location but typically takes 10-14 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free within the US. To start a return, visit your account page or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. If you can't find your tracking information, please contact our support team.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections. We do not store your payment information.

How do I change or cancel my order? +

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After this time, orders enter processing and cannot be changed. To modify or cancel an order, contact our support team immediately or use the "Cancel Order" option in your account if available.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. International customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes. You can see exact shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.
