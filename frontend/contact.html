Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping: 5-7 business days within the US. Express shipping: 2-3 business days. International shipping: 10-14 business days depending on the destination. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free within the US. International returns may incur shipping costs. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see exact shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.

How do I track my order? +

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

How do I become a Trendaryo Circle member? +

You can join the Trendaryo Circle by signing up on our homepage or during checkout. Membership is free and gives you access to early product drops, exclusive discounts, and member-only content.
