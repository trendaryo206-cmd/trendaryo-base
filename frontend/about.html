How Trendaryo became the destination for curated trends

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a small team of trend enthusiasts frustrated with endless online shopping. We believed there had to be a better way — one that combined quality curation with personalized discovery.

  • Started in a small apartment office
  • First 100 products handpicked by founders
  • Launched with 50 beta users
2021

Finding Our Voice

Developed our unique curation algorithm and established relationships with emerging brands. Grew our community to 1,000+ trend hunters.

  • Launched Trendaryo Circle membership

  • Expanded team to 10 members
2022

Rapid Growth

Scaled operations while maintaining our commitment to quality. Introduced personalized collections and expanded product categories.

  • Reached 10,000+ customers
  • Launched international shipping
  • Featured in major lifestyle publications

Today & Beyond

Continuing to innovate while staying true to our core mission: making trend discovery effortless, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone.
